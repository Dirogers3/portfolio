Notes from the video

  • Mobile websites have skyrocketed, basically replacing computers.
  • Computers are replaced ever 4-5 years while phones are 2 years. People have 2-3 devices per person.
  • Mobile first for websites, while time consuming it gives a better experience and help with bandwidth.
  • Be there, Be useful, and be quick. Use these rules when you cream your applications.
  • Reach ability on the mobile phone with a one handed grip.
  • Speed maters: Reduce image usage and use SVG/Webfonts, Optimize and Minify CSS and JS files, use GZIP files eliminate not needed data.
  • Most commonly used items should be at the bottom of the screen or where people can reach with one hand.
  • Hamburger menu isn't generally known. But using the word "menu" with a border has the highest amount of clicks
  • Enable auto complete on forms if possible.
  • Make phone number clickable to call when having on mobile websites.
  • Keep ads unintrusive and simple.
